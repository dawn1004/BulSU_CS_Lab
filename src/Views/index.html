Mission of BulSU

Bulacan State University exists to produce highly competent, ethical and service-oriented professionals that contribute to the sustainable socio-economic growth and development of the nation

History of College of Science

    The College of Science evolved from the College of Arts and Science (CAS), which offered the course Bachelor of Science in Mathematics major in Computer Science in 1998, followed by Bachelor of Science in Biology in 1999.
    In March 2005, the College of Arts and Science was separated into various independent entities, one of which was the Institute of Science (IS). In June 2008, the Institute added two more fields of specialization under the BS Mathematics program namely: Business Applications and Applied Statistics.
    By virtue of BOR #66, series of 2008, the Board of Regents, during their 4th Regular meeting on December 2, 2008 approved the conversion of the Institute of Science into the College of Science. In 2013, two new courses namely BS Environmental Science and BS Food Science (now BS Food Technology) were offered.

Vision of College of Science

    The College of Science aspires to be recognized as a center of excellence for learning where human talents and skills are honed to their fullest potentials in an environment that promotes highest academic standards and cultural values.

Mission of College of Science

    The College of Science will devote its human and material resources to improve knowledge, develop skills, cultivate values, conduct research studies, and facilitate extension promotive of the goals of nation-building and lofty human aspirations in the fields of sciences and mathematics.
